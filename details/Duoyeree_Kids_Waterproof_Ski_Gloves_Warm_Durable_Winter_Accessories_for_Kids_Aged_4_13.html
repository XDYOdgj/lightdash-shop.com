Duoyeree Kids Gloves – Stay Warm, Stay Dry

These Waterproof Ski Gloves are crafted to keep your kids’ hands cozy and protected during the colder months. With strong waterproof and insulating properties, they’re the perfect winter accessory for children ages 4-13.
