Elope's Train Conductor Costume Hat

This train conductor hat is the perfect choice for little boys aged 5 to 12 who love to wear their imagination on their head. Designed with a faux leather bill and a foam sculpted conductor plate, it combines style with comfort for all-day wear. The adjustable fit ensures it stays secure and looks great no matter how adventurous your child gets.

Key Features

  • Perfect for costume parties and role-play as train conductors
  • 100% polyester construction for ultimate comfort and durability
  • Reversible bill offers a customizable fit and versatile style
  • Foam sculpted conductor plate adds realistic detail and ensures a snug fit
  • Easy size adjustment with built-in strap for a secure and personalized look

Why Choose This Hat?

Whether your little one is preparing for a themed party or playing pretend at home, this conductor hat is designed to make them feel like the real deal. Its soft and breathable material keeps them cool and comfortable, while the adjustable strap ensures the hat fits perfectly on any head shape. The reverse design is a bonus, allowing for extra style variety without compromising on comfort or functionality.

Ideal For

A great addition to any costume collection, this train conductor hat is ideal for boys who enjoy imaginative play and special events. Its light and flexible design makes it easy to wear and move around in, ensuring that your child can focus on having fun instead of adjusting their gear.
